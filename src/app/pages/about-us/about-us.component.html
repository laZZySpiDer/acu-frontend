The Story Behind Ahiru

Before ACU, I was a Genetics student.

Between lab work and textbooks, I found comfort in poetry, doodles, and storytelling through art.

In 2017, a little character named Ahiru was born and she became the soul of my creative world. Friends began connecting with her, asking for drawings, badges, and personalised pieces. What started as handmade gifts soon turned into my first customised orders and eventually, ACU.
